How it works

Three steps.
Zero context switches.

Bento runs on a fully isolated virtual desktop. You never see it working — until it's done.

01

Type your task

Open Bento from the menubar and describe what you need in plain English. No commands, no syntax.

02

Bento disappears

He salutes, runs off screen, and handles it on a separate virtual desktop — never touching yours.

03

He comes back

When it's done, Bento returns with the result. File on your clipboard. Music playing. Task complete.
